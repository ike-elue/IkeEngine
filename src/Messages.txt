Special Messages (Off limits unless used with real purpose):
"" = name of component
"collider" = sends to collision_world
"render" = sends to renderer
"camera_coords" = sends camera coordinates to renderer
"init_scene" = message that happens when scene starts
"physics_world_gravity" = sets physics world's gravity to associated data in form of Vector2 (Not Vector2f)
"physics_body" = sends physics body to physics world
